# Privacy Policy — TibiaTimer

**Last updated: 2026-06-29**

## Data Collection

TibiaTimer does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All settings and configurations are saved locally on your device only.

## Automatic Updates

The application uses [Velopack](https://velopack.io) to check for new versions. This connects solely to GitHub Releases (`github.com/galvaodev/timertibia`) to compare the current version with the latest available release. No personal information is sent during this process.

## Third-Party Services

TibiaTimer does not integrate with any analytics, telemetry, advertising, or tracking services.
